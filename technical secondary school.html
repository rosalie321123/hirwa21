Software Development Department

Building Future Programmers and Innovators

Overview

The Software Development Department in a Technical Secondary School equips students with practical and theoretical knowledge in programming, system design, and digital technology. It prepares learners for careers in the IT industry or further studies in computer science.

Core Subjects

  • Computer science
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Mathematics

Objectives

  • Develop strong coding skills
  • Prepare students for software careers
  • Encourage innovation and problem-solving
  • Promote digital entrepreneurship

Skills Developed

  • Web and app development
  • Debugging and problem-solving
  • Team collaboration (projects)
  • Logical thinking

Teaching Methods

  • Hands-on coding practice
  • Real-world projects
  • Group collaboration
  • Internships and industrial training

Career Opportunities

  • Web Developer
  • Software Engineer
  • IT Support Technician
  • Freelancer / Entrepreneur
