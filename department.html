Nursery

Nursery Department

Building Strong Foundations for Young Learners

About Nursery

The nursery department is the first stage of formal education for children aged 3–5 years. It introduces basic learning skills in a friendly and playful environment.


Objectives

  • Develop language and communication skills
  • Introduce basic counting and shapes
  • Improve social interaction and teamwork
  • Support emotional and physical growth

Subjects

  • Literacy
  • Numeracy
  • sports

Learning Methods

We use fun and interactive methods such as songs, storytelling, drawing, group games, and learning through play.


Facilities

  • Colorful classrooms
  • Safe playground
  • Learning toys and books
  • Child-friendly furniture

Importance

Nursery education builds a strong foundation for future academic success, improves confidence, and develops essential life skills.

Primary

Primary Department

Teaching the public studies for primary learners

Overview

The Primary Department provides foundational education for learners from Primary 1 to Primary 6. It focuses on developing academic skills, character, and social abilities in a supportive environment.

Academic Program

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science

Teaching Approach

Lessons are interactive and learner-centered, using group work, games, and practical activities to encourage creativity and critical thinking.

Moral & Social Development

  • Discipline and responsibility
  • Respect for others
  • Teamwork and cooperation
  • Good values and behavior

Co-Curricular Activities

  • Sports and physical education
  • Music, dance, and drama
  • Art and craft
  • School clubs

Assessment

Students are evaluated through continuous assessment, tests, and exams. Feedback helps them improve and succeed academically.

Software Development Department

Building Future Programmers and Innovators

Overview

The Software Development Department in a Technical Secondary School equips students with practical and theoretical knowledge in programming, system design, and digital technology. It prepares learners for careers in the IT industry or further studies in computer science.

Core Subjects

  • Entrepreneurship
  • Mathematics

Objectives

  • Develop strong coding skills
  • Prepare students for software careers
  • Encourage innovation and problem-solving

  • Team collaboration (projects)

Teaching Methods

  • Real-world projects

Career Opportunities
