Nursery Department

Building Strong Foundations for Young Learners

About Nursery

The nursery department is the first stage of formal education for children aged 3–5 years. It introduces basic learning skills in a friendly and playful environment.


Objectives

  • Develop language and communication skills
  • Introduce basic counting and shapes
  • Improve social interaction and teamwork
  • Support emotional and physical growth

Subjects

  • Literacy
  • Numeracy
  • sports

Learning Methods

We use fun and interactive methods such as songs, storytelling, drawing, group games, and learning through play.


Facilities

  • Colorful classrooms
  • Safe playground
  • Learning toys and books
  • Child-friendly furniture

Importance

Nursery education builds a strong foundation for future academic success, improves confidence, and develops essential life skills.
